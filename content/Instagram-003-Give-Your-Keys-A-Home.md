# One Eco Step: Give Your Keys a Home

## Status

Draft v1.0

## Purpose

Turn ETLH's "reduce unnecessary friction" principle into one small, practical home habit that readers can do today.

## Format

Instagram carousel, 5 slides, 4:5 vertical.

## Caption

One small source of friction is easy to ignore.

Until it repeats every day.

Looking for your keys, wallet, badge, or bag might seem like a tiny thing — but tiny things still use attention.

Your Eco Step:
Choose one permanent home for your keys today.

A bowl.
A hook.
A tray.
A pocket in your everyday bag.

The goal is not to organize your whole life.

Just remove one repeated frustration.

Make tomorrow a little lighter than today. 🌿
