# Less Friction, Less Waste

## Status

Draft v1.0

## Purpose

Show how reducing unnecessary friction can also reduce waste by making sustainable choices easier to repeat.

## Format

Instagram carousel, 5 slides, 4:5 vertical.

## Carousel Script

### Slide 1

Headline:
Less friction.
Less waste.

Body:
Sustainable habits are easier when your home supports them.

Visual Direction:
Calm small kitchen or entryway with reusable items placed clearly and naturally.

### Slide 2

Headline:
Most waste is not just a values problem.

Body:
Sometimes it is a system problem.

Visual Direction:
Small kitchen counter with reusable bag, lunch container, mug, and a few everyday items.

### Slide 3

Headline:
If the better choice is hard to reach,
you will skip it when life gets busy.

Body:
That does not mean you failed.
It means the system needs help.

Visual Direction:
Reusable bag or bottle tucked away versus one placed visibly near the door or counter.

### Slide 4

Headline:
Make the sustainable choice obvious.

Body:
Put reusable bags by the door.
Keep a water bottle ready.
Store containers where you actually pack food.

Visual Direction:
Simple small-space setup showing reusable bags, bottle, and containers in easy-to-reach places.

### Slide 5

Headline:
Your Eco Step

Body:
Move one reusable item to the place you naturally need it.

Less searching.
Less forgetting.
Less waste.

Visual Direction:
Peaceful small home scene with one reusable item placed intentionally where it will be used.

## Caption

Sustainable habits are easier when your home supports them.

Sometimes waste does not happen because you do not care.

It happens because the better choice is hidden, inconvenient, or one step too far away when life gets busy.

That is unnecessary friction.

Your Eco Step:
Move one reusable item to the place you naturally need it.

Put reusable bags by the door.
Keep your water bottle ready.
Store food containers where you actually pack food.

Small systems make thoughtful choices easier to repeat.

Less friction.
Less waste. 🌿
