# Small Space Reset: Clear One Surface

## Status

Published

## Purpose

Give readers one quick, practical reset that reduces visual clutter and creates a small feeling of calm in a small space.

## Format

Instagram carousel, 5 slides, 4:5 vertical.

## Carousel Script

### Slide 1

Headline:
Small Space Reset:
Clear one surface.

Body:

Visual Direction:

### Slide 2

Headline:
Visual clutter asks for attention.

Body:
Even when you are not using it,
your brain still notices what is unfinished.

Visual Direction:
A small counter or desk with mail, mug, notebook, phone, and a few loose items.

### Slide 3

Headline:
Choose one surface.

Body:
Not the whole apartment.
Not every drawer.

Visual Direction:

### Slide 4

Headline:
Reset it in three steps.

Body:
Remove what does not belong.
Group what stays.
Leave one clear space.

Visual Direction:

### Slide 5

Headline:
Your Eco Step

Visual Direction:
Peaceful small home scene with one clean surface, warm light, and a calm finished feeling.

## Caption

A small space can feel heavy when every surface is asking for attention.

You do not need to clean the whole room.

Start smaller.

Choose one surface:
a table,
a counter,
a nightstand,
a desk,
or the spot by the door.

Remove what does not belong.
Group what stays.
Leave one clear space.

That small reset can make the whole room feel lighter.

Your Eco Step:
Clear one surface today.

Let your space give a little attention back. 🌿
