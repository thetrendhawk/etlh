## Caption

A small home can still feel calm.

Calm is not about having more space.

It is about reducing what works against you.

You do not need a perfect home.
You need a home that supports the way you actually live.

That might start with one simple system:
a place for your keys,
a clear surface,
a bag ready by the door,
or one repeated frustration made easier.

Small spaces do not need to feel heavy.

Your Eco Step:
Choose one small place to make easier today.

Let your home give a little calm back. 🌿
