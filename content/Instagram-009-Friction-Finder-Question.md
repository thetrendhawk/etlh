# The Friction Finder Question

## Status

Published

## Purpose

Introduce the Friction Finder Question as a repeatable ETLH tool for noticing small sources of unnecessary friction and choosing one simple improvement.

## Caption

One better question can change how you see your home:

What keeps asking for my attention without giving much back?

That is the Friction Finder Question.

Friction usually hides in small repeated moments.

The thing you keep moving.
The item you keep losing.
The task you keep avoiding.
The surface that keeps collecting things.

You do not have to fix everything.

Start with what repeats.

Give it a home.
Move it closer.
Remove one step.
Write it down.

Your Eco Step:
Tonight, ask yourself:

What can I make easier so tomorrow feels lighter? 🌿

Save this for your next small reset.
