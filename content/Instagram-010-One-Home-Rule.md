## Caption

A lot of daily friction comes from small things that never quite land anywhere.

Keys that wander.
A reusable bag that ends up in a different room.
A charger you keep looking for.
Mail that keeps moving from one surface to another.

That is where the One-Home Rule can help.

If you keep looking for something, it probably needs one obvious home.

Not a perfect system.
Not a full reset.
Just one clear place you can return to without thinking.

A bowl by the door.
A hook at eye level.
A basket where you naturally drop it.

Small systems reduce friction.
And less friction makes calm easier to repeat. 🌿
