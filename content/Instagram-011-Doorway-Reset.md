## Caption

The doorway is one of the easiest places for friction to build.

Keys land wherever.
Bags get dropped.
Shoes pile up.
Mail moves from one surface to another.

And then leaving the house feels harder than it needs to.

The Doorway Reset is simple:
give the doorway one helpful job.

It should help you leave easier,
come home calmer,
and stop losing the same things.

A bowl for keys.
A hook for bags.
A tray for mail.
A place for shoes.

You do not need a perfect entryway.
You just need one small station that supports real life.

Your Eco Step:
Before tonight ends, choose one doorway item and give it one easy place to land. 🌿
