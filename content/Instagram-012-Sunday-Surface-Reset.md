## Caption

A Sunday reset does not need to mean cleaning the whole apartment.

Start smaller.

Choose one surface.

The table.
The counter.
The nightstand.
The desk.
The spot by the door.

Small surfaces collect attention quickly.

Mail, cups, keys, notes, and little unfinished things can make a small home feel heavier than it needs to.

Try this:

Remove what does not belong.
Group what stays.
Leave one clear space.

That is enough.

Your Eco Step:
Clear one surface today so tomorrow starts a little lighter. 🌿
