# ETLH Instagram Content Queue

## Status

Active planning and publication-tracking document

## Purpose

Track published and upcoming Instagram posts so ETLH content is planned intentionally instead of improvised.

## Publishing Principles

- Every post should help the reader feel lighter, clearer, or more capable.
- Every post should connect to one ETLH idea: less friction, less waste, less overwhelm, more room for what matters.
- Prefer small practical actions over broad inspiration.
- Keep the tone calm, useful, and non-judgmental.
- Do not post just to post.

## Status Definitions

- **Published:** Final carousel package completed and posted to Instagram.
- **Provisional:** Approved planning direction carried into migration materials, but no production file or completed asset package exists in the repository yet.
