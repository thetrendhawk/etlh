# ETLH Search-Content Brief Template

## Purpose

Use this template only after a topic has passed the ETLH Search Research Methodology. It converts a traceable evidence packet into an executable article plan without inventing demand, traffic, ranking potential, reader experiences, claims, credentials, analytics, or owner decisions.

A completed brief is an editorial control document. It is not proof that a topic will rank or convert.

# 13. Quality and publication gates

## Research gate

- [ ] Direct evidence is traceable.
- [ ] Search intent and result-page review are dated.
- [ ] Existing ETLH coverage was checked.
- [ ] Cluster role is stated without inventing owner approval.

## Editorial gate

- [ ] The article promise is specific and supportable.
- [ ] Scope and exclusions are explicit.
- [ ] Consequential claims have authoritative support.
- [ ] Uncertainty, tradeoffs, and limitations are visible.
- [ ] The article gives the reader a practical outcome.

## Trust gate

- [ ] No quotation, statistic, demand claim, ranking claim, credential, test result, savings claim, or owner fact was invented.
- [ ] Affiliate or commercial relationships are disclosed where applicable.
- [ ] Product recommendations are based on stated criteria and evidence.
- [ ] Images do not create false evidence.

## Search and technical gate

- [ ] Title, description, canonical, headings, and slug are aligned with the primary intent.
- [ ] Internal links are useful and non-duplicative.
- [ ] Structured data matches visible content and eligibility rules.
- [ ] Images have dimensions, responsive handling, and appropriate alt text.
- [ ] Content, route, sitemap, accessibility, and production-build checks pass.

## Owner-decision gate

- [ ] Priority and publication approval are recorded when required.
- [ ] Legal, paid, destructive, credentialed, or owner-only decisions are not inferred.

## Definition of ready for drafting

A brief is ready only when:

- its research packet is traceable
- the reader problem and primary intent are clear
- existing coverage and cannibalization were checked
- the article’s cluster role is stated
- the promise, scope, exclusions, and practical outcome are explicit
- every consequential claim has a source plan
- internal links, metadata, schema, and images are planned
- unresolved uncertainty and owner decisions are visible
- no demand, ranking, traffic, conversion, credential, quotation, or factual claim has been invented
