Prepared for owner use only. Do not publish or generate images.

## 1. Visual Batch Prompt

Background-only vertical small-kitchen images with uncluttered text-safe negative space. No appliance product, logo, endorsement, compost claim, measurement, or documentary cue.

## 2. Copy Template

1. A countertop appliance is not automatically a compost system.
2. Name the process: heat, grind, dry, or something else.
3. Plan the output route first.
4. Check manual, capacity, cleaning, filters, noise, and warranty.
5. Check counter space, outlet, ventilation, building rules, and household fit.
6. Do not buy is a valid conclusion.

## 3. Caption + Hashtags

```text
Before comparing countertop food-waste appliances, ask what happens after the cycle ends.

An appliance can treat scraps without creating finished compost. Check the exact manual, output route, cleaning, filters, noise, space, electricity, building rules, and warranty terms before buying.

If the whole route does not fit your apartment, pause.

```
