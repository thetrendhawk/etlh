# Instagram package — Food labels and storage

**Format:** Locked 5-panel carousel; owner review required before any external posting.

1. Keep the original package when it fits and the information matters.
2. A secondary label can identify food, but cannot recreate every ingredient, allergen, date, lot, or direction field.
3. A quality date is not, by itself, a complete food-safety decision.
4. Check current package, manufacturer, FDA, USDA, or FoodKeeper guidance for the specific question.
5. Try one no-purchase information check; stopping or keeping the original package is valid.

**Caption:** Small kitchens can separate food from the information that came with it. This guide keeps the focus on information preservation—not a pantry makeover, medical advice, or universal packaging rule.
