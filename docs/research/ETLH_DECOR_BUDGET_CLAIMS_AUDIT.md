## Selection rationale

This is the highest-risk remaining article after the completed kitchen and eco-step checklist repairs. It presents unverified personal experience, ranks marketplaces as universal winners, makes an unsupported $200 plant comparison and extreme survival claim, labels adhesive products renter-safe, and omits lease, surface, load, pet/child, pest, recall, transport, accessibility, and total-cost limits. Its source hero also contains a visible Midjourney mark and an unverified “thrifted” alt claim.

- The repaired room-reset and eco-step checklist articles provide use-existing, measurement, lease, surface, load, accessibility, and household decision boundaries.

## Evidence missing

- No market comparison supports a universal sourcing winner or lower total cost.
- No itemized evidence supports the $200 comparison.
- No source establishes universal plant survival, renter safety, adhesive removability, or tension-rod load suitability.
- No lifecycle evidence proves an item or sourcing channel is environmentally preferable overall.
