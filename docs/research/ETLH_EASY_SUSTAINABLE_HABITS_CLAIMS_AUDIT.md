# 20 Renter-Aware Sustainable Habits — Claims Audit

**Route:** `/blog/easy-sustainable-habits-on-a-budget`  
**Audit date:** 2026-07-20  
**Scope:** source article, numbered-list integrity, presentation override, metadata/card/schema inputs, body claims, internal links, and associated promotional image.

## Reader purpose

The article can remain a broad renter-oriented planning page, but it should not promise that sustainable habits are easy, cheap, savings-producing, or universally controllable. The repaired purpose is to help a reader identify one no-purchase or use-what-you-have option, check safety/access/lease/household/local constraints, and decide whether to keep, change, or stop.

## Numbered-list decision

The source contains 20 distinct items, not 21: 3 starting principles, 4 energy items, 4 water/cleaning items, 4 shopping/reusable items, and 5 food/waste items. The honest repair changes the title and all public surfaces to **20**. The article does not present completion of all 20 as a goal and does not imply a 21-day habit timeline.
