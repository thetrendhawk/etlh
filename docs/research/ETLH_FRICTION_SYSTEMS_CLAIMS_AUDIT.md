# ETLH Friction Systems Claims Audit

**Route:** `/blog/why-life-feels-harder-than-it-needs-to-sometimes`  
**Audit date:** 2026-07-20  
**Decision:** Repair in place; retain the intentional-living purpose and URL.

## Independent reader purpose

The page has a defensible purpose distinct from the renter checklist, broad habits pages, and apartment-systems control map: it helps a reader observe recurring friction in an existing routine and test one small, reversible arrangement change. It is not a sustainability outcome guide, a mental-health intervention, or a behavior-science evidence summary.

## Claim disposition

| Surface or claim family | Classification | Repair |
| --- | --- | --- |
| “lighter, clearer, easier to manage,” “heavy,” “crowded” | narrow | Present as possible reader experience or observation, not a promised result. |
| “daily stress,” “take more energy,” “calmer,” “compassion for real energy” | remove or narrow | Use “friction,” “extra steps,” and “attention” as observable routine descriptions; avoid health or emotional outcomes. |
| “one small adjustment could make the whole day feel lighter” | replace with process guidance | Ask the reader to test, keep, change, or stop based on what they notice. |

| “Why We’re Confident in This Advice” behavior-change claim | remove | No approved authoritative behavior-design research is needed when the evidence-style section is removed. |
| Specific placement suggestions | preserve with constraints | Keep as optional, no-purchase examples; add access, household, lease, sanitation, and local-service caveats where relevant. |
| Source image `SustainablePin1.webp` and presentation `cat-habits.jpg` | replace/retire | Align source to the existing illustrative checklist image and retire the now-unused promotional source asset from emitted output, preserving it in repository source. |

## Overlap check

The renter checklist and habits pages answer “which option can I try?” The apartment-systems page answers “who controls this system?” This page answers “where is a repeated routine adding steps, and how can I test one arrangement change?” Links will make those adjacent paths explicit without repeating their full structures.

## Evidence limits

No analytics, search-volume, ranking-difficulty, mental-health, productivity, stress, habit-formation, savings, waste, or environmental evidence is available or inferred. The repaired article is an editorial observation exercise, not a measured intervention or medical guidance.
