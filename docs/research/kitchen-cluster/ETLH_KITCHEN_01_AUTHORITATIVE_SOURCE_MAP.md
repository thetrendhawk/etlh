# ETLH-KITCHEN-01 authoritative source-to-claim map

Verified: 2026-07-19

## Scope

This map supports the in-place refresh of `/blog/zero-waste-kitchen-ideas-tiny-apartments`. It separates food-scrap **collection and staging** from compost **processing**. A local collection program's rules remain controlling for accepted materials, liners, and transfer instructions.

Publication or update dates are recorded when the source displays one. “Not stated” means no reliable publication date was visible; the verification date above is the retrieval date.

## Claim controls

- Say freezer staging **slows** microbial activity and may reduce odor or fly access; never say it sterilizes scraps or guarantees a pest-free container.
- Apply moisture/aeration/browns guidance only to an active backyard or worm-compost system. A collection pail is a transfer container, not a miniature compost pile.
- Describe bokashi as fermentation and its output as pre-compost requiring a verified finishing route.
- Use local examples only as examples. Readers must verify current accepted materials, liners, access, and transfer rules with their actual destination.
- Do not publish legal, tenant-rights, health, capacity, prevalence, or product-performance claims from these sources.

## Firsthand evidence boundary

Records `C1-001`–`C1-018` and the selected `N-*` records establish reader friction and vocabulary only. They do not establish technical causes, prevalence, manufacturer performance, or a universal best method. `N-022` remains corroboration-only.
